## 📊 Executive Summary

LenaBeauty is a comprehensive salon management system built for the Omani market. This roadmap outlines **15+ premium features** that will significantly enhance the product's value proposition and competitive advantage.

## 🎯 Phase 1: Core Revenue Features (Q3 2026)

### 1.1 **Advanced Loyalty Program** ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium | **ROI**: Excellent

Implement a sophisticated loyalty system that goes beyond simple point tracking:

**Features**:
- **Tiered Membership**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
- **Automatic Tier Progression**: Based on spending
- **Exclusive Perks**: Discounts, free services, birthday specials
- **Referral Rewards**: Customers earn points for referrals
- **Expiring Points**: Encourage spending (points expire after 12 months)
- **Redemption Options**: Services, products, or cash discount
- **Mobile Wallet**: QR code for easy point redemption

**Business Value**:
- Increase customer retention by 40%
- Average customer lifetime value increases by 60%
- Encourages repeat visits

**Pricing**: Include in Professional & Enterprise plans
